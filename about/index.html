Overview

Welcome to the Edinburgh University Trading and Investment Club (EUTIC). Celebrating 22 years of experience, EUTIC is now the largest and most successful student-run real money investment portfolio in the UK and Europe, worth over Â£58,000. We also have over 500 members, making us one of the largest non-sporting societies in Edinburgh.

100+ Events. Every Semester.

General Meetings

EUTIC holds a weekly club-wide meeting that gives members the opportunity to learn more about finance and current affairs. The line-up includes fund updates, debates, educational talks, as well as external and internal speakers. Join us on Mondays, 6:30 at David Hume Tower Lecture Theatre A.

Sector Meetings

The EUTIC Fund is managed by 9 dedicated sectors, each focusing on a different research area. By joining a sector you can get involved in managing the fund, looking into an industry that you're passionate about. EUTIC provides you with a unique opportunity to learn about investment at no personal risk as well as meeting new people. Drop by one of the weekly sector meetings and join the conversation!

Sponsor Events

EUTIC is lucky to have a fantastic range of industry-leading sponsors, each with a commitment to attracting the best talent to their firms. By attending our sponsor events, including presentations, networking events, insight days, and competitions throughout the year, you will be able to meet professionals and find out more about possible gradate employment opportunities.

Pitches

Our members actively participate in the investment of the EUTIC fund by undertaking roles as analysts in a given research sector. This gives you the chance to develop key technical skills through the research of potential investments, analysis of company information, and interpretation of the current economic tendencies. Stocks and bonds are then pitched to our investment panel comprised of committee members and industry professionals.

Career Workshops

We promote events such as CV improvement workshops, networking events, company presentations and alumni talks for members insterested in pursuing a career in the financial services industry. In fact, more than 65% of our members have secured spring weeks and/or internships in the financial services industry.

Socials

EUTIC, with 9 sectors, an annual conference, and various networking events, gives you the perfect chance to meet other students as well as professionals. Our social events throughout the year, such as our Christmas Banquet and Annual Gala are a great place to relax and celebrate the work done throughout the year.
