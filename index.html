Edinburgh University Trading & Investment Club
Established in 1998, we are among the UK’s largest student-managed investment funds.

400 members

£58,000 Assets Under Management

22 Years of Experience

Our Mission

Our objectives are to educate students about the world of finance, give members real-life experience in trading stocks, provide opportunities to network with some ofthe world’s most prestigious firms, and help members pursue careers in the financial services industry and other leading graduate recruiters.

What We Do

Main Meetings

EUTIC holds Monday main meetings – the clubs main attraction, which give members a chance to learn more about finance in the form of talks given by outside speakers, often from major sponsors from the City. Club members are also invited to give talks on finance-related topics. This is a great chance for you to improve on your research skills, and EUTIC members are one of the best audiences in the university.

Sectors

The EUTIC fund is managed by 10 dedicated sectors. Within sector meetings you will gain more knowledge about the specific industry sector you choose to enter. This will give you more confidence in analysing and picking stocks and, ultimately, allow you to influence the performance of the EUTIC fund.

Socials

EUTIC offers some of the best - attended social events in the university, with the aim of building a strong community around our society. Join one of our socials to have the time of your life and meet like minded individuals who could become your closest friends at university.

Become a Member

Get your membership online, from us at EUSA’s Activities Fair during Fresher’s Week, or at any of our weekly meetings.

An annual EUTIC membership costs £5 for any matriculated University of Edinburgh student, and £10 for non-students. Semester memberships are also available.
